## FAQ for Comments

**Q: How is this different from Ollama Web UI?**
A: We focused on UX polish, one-click installs, and marketplace discovery. Think "App Store for AI models."

**Q: What hardware do I need?**
A: Minimum: 8GB RAM, works CPU-only. Recommended: 16GB RAM + GPU for faster inference.

**Q: Can I use my own models?**
A: Yes! Any Ollama-compatible model works. We just make discovery/install easier.

**Q: Is this really free?**
A: Yes, 100% free and open source. All models are open source too.

**Q: What about fine-tuning?**
A: Not yet, but it's on the roadmap! For now, focused on chat inference.

**Q: Windows/Mac/Linux?**
A: All three! Runs via Docker, or native if you prefer.
